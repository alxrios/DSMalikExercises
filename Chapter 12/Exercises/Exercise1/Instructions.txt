
Write a program that converts a number entered in Roman numerals to decimal.
Your program should consist of a class, say romanType. An object of the type
romanType should do the following:

a. Store the number as a Roman numeral.

b. Convert and store the number into decimal.

c. Print the number as a Roman numeral or decimal number as requested by the user.

   The decimal values of the Roman numerals are:

   M 1000
   D  500
   C  100
   L   50
   X   10
   V    5
   I    1

d. Test your program using the following Roman numerals: MCXIV, CCCLIX, MDCLXVI.
